About Us

UniSched USM is the ultimate scheduling solution designed to meet the unique needs of all USM students. We aim to make your
academic planning simple and efficient, so you can focus on your studies and make the most of your university experience.


Our platform offers a comprehensive suite of features to streamline your academic journey:

Personalized Timetables

Effortlessly create class timetables
with class slots for multiple courses as
shown in the school's master schedule.

Flexible Adjustments

Add, edit, or remove slots for tutorials
and lab sessions to suit your schedule.

Conflict Alerts

Receive real-time alerts to avoid
scheduling conflicts and ensure a
smooth timetable coordination.

Downloadable Timetables

Save and print your finalized
timetables in convenient
PDF formats.

Course and Lecturer Information

Access essential information about
courses in USM and the
lecturers in charge.

Campus Map

Easily navigate the USM campus
with our static map feature.
